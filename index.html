The Project

The Project

Comedy

Stand-up comedians will entertain with humor on everyday life and societal issues, while skits and improvisation will bring spontaneous laughter and audience interaction.

Music

Singers, instrumentalists, and bands will perform diverse genres, with opportunities for original compositions and audience sing-alongs to create a dynamic musical experience.

Acting

Participants will present plays, monologues, and short films, showcasing their dramatic abilities while engaging the audience with behind-the-scenes storytelling.

Modeling

Aspiring models will shine on the runway with diverse styles, participate in themed competitions, and learn from workshops on confidence and presentation.

Spoken Word and Poetry

Poets and spoken word artists will perform at open mics and poetry slams, with workshops and themed showcases to inspire creativity and deep conversations.

Screen Filming

Short film screenings, workshops on filmmaking, and a "48-hour film challenge" will encourage creativity, storytelling, and collaboration among participants.

Art Exhibitions

Art displays will include paintings, photography, and digital art, with live demonstrations, collaborative projects, and opportunities to purchase or commission artwork.

Workshops and Mentorship

Interactive sessions will build skills in public speaking and stage presence, while mentorship and networking will connect participants with industry experts.

Audience Engagement

Attendees will vote for their favorite acts, participate in talent challenges, and connect with performers through meet-and-greet opportunities and social media-friendly spaces.

About us

About us

About Us

We are a passionate group of students from the University of Eldoret actively involved in co-curricular activities, particularly the drama club. Our vision is to create impactful projects that not only entertain but also encourage creative expression.

  • Talent Showcase

    The event serves as a platform for individuals to display their unique skills, whether in music, comedy, acting, modeling, or screen filming. Each performance is an opportunity to connect with the audience emotionally, inspiring and entertaining them.

  • Collaboration

    Participants will interact with fellow talents, sharing ideas, experiences, and insights. This collaboration fosters teamwork and builds long-lasting friendships

  • Networking Opportunities

    The festival offers a chance for performers to meet industry professionals, mentors, and potential sponsors, creating valuable connections for future growth.

Our Objective

Our Objective

Discover Hidden Talents

The project aims to uncover and showcase unique abilities in individuals, providing a stage for talents that might otherwise go unnoticed. By creating a welcoming platform, it seeks to encourage participants from all backgrounds to step forward, express themselves, and explore their potential. This objective emphasizes inclusivity and diversity, ensuring every participant has an equal chance to shine.

Nurture and Develop Skills

Beyond identifying talent, the festival focuses on skill-building and growth. Through workshops, mentorship programs, and constructive feedback, participants will have the opportunity to refine their abilities. This objective promotes personal development by equipping individuals with the tools and knowledge they need to excel in their chosen creative fields.

Promote Cultural Exchange

The event fosters cultural diversity by featuring performances and expressions from different traditions, styles, and perspectives. It provides a platform for participants and audiences to celebrate their heritage while learning about others. This objective strengthens community bonds and encourages unity through the shared experience of creativity.

Provide Networking Opportunities

The festival seeks to connect emerging talents with professionals, mentors, and industry leaders. By creating spaces for interaction, it helps participants establish valuable relationships that can lead to career growth and future collaborations. This objective ensures the festival acts as a bridge between budding artists and opportunities in their respective industries.

Engage and Inspire the Community

The event aims to engage the broader community by offering an entertaining, educational, and interactive experience. By involving audiences as active participants, it inspires them to support local talent and appreciate the power of creativity. This objective helps build a supportive and enthusiastic environment that celebrates artistic expression.

Create a Sustainable Talent Platform

The project envisions establishing a long-term platform for talents to grow and thrive. By organizing annual events, nurturing partnerships, and maintaining an inclusive culture, the festival seeks to leave a lasting impact. This objective ensures that future generations will continue to benefit from a system that recognizes and supports artistic potential.

Our Team

Our team is composed of passionate and dedicated individuals who are committed to discovering and nurturing diverse talents. We come from various backgrounds and disciplines, bringing a wealth of experience and unique perspectives to the project. Our team members include students, professionals, and volunteers, all united by a shared belief in the power of creativity and the arts to bring people together and make a positive impact on society.

Frequently Asked Questions

We accept a wide range of talents, including comedy, music, acting, dance, modeling, spoken word, poetry, screen filming, and other creative performances. We encourage diverse forms of expression.

Winners will be chosen based on a combination of criteria, including creativity, talent, stage presence, audience engagement, and overall impact. A panel of judges will score performances, and audience votes may also contribute.
